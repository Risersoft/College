# Notice Board

## Create

To create noticeboard, click on **About College -> NoticeBoard -> New NoticeBoard**.

![](/images/newnoticeboard.png)
 
Form will appear. Fill the details and click on **Save**.

![](/images/noticeboardform.png)
 
## List

To see the list of noticeboards, click on **NoticeBoard -> List of NoticeBoard**.

![](/images/noticeboardlist.png)
 
## Edit

To edit the noticeboard, **right click on Noticeboard -> Edit NoticeBoard**.

![](/images/editnoticeboard.png)
